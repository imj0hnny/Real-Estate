"Don’t just dream it, own it."

"Expert guidance for every square foot of your journey."

Securing a home is a monumental milestone that defines your everyday life, so we better make sure it's a masterpiece. It requires a vision, a deep level of trust, and a professional who won’t let you make a tragic mistake. As a Premier Real Estate Agent and a Master Hair Stylist (scroll to “Salon Inquiry” section), I’m that rare partner who can negotiate a killer closing credit and tell you exactly which shade of paint will make your foyer (and your skin tone) pop.

I’m a North Carolina coastal native who brought my salt-water soul to North Hills, where I live with my partner, Hunter, and our two honorary "branch managers," Blayden and Brody (our very spoiled puppies). I’m a total beach bum at heart, and when I’m not obsessing over floor plans, you’ll find me jumping waves at the coast or back in town with a bone-dry martini in hand, ready to gab and find the laughter in everything.

Why work with me? I’m a perfectionist who actually enjoys the challenging side of a transaction. I’ll handle the gritty negotiations, the fine-print headaches, and the stressful logistics so you can focus on the fun stuff—like where the bar cart goes. I’m kind, I’m hardworking, and I’m your biggest cheerleader. When you win, we win together, and we’re going to have a blast getting there!

I believe life is too short for boring houses, bad hair, or high-stress agents. I’m here to make your closing process as pleasant and refreshing as a salon day. Let’s get to work and find you the dream home (and the dream hair) you deserve!

With a career built on education and community, I thrive on helping others reach their full potential.
